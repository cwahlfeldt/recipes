Sugar cookies

Ingredients

  • 3½ cups of flour
  • 1t of backing powder
  • salt
  • 1½ cups of sugar
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 1½ of vanilla

Directions

Cream butter and sugar then add the beaten eggs and vanilla + the rest of the ingredients. Bake at 400° on a greased or non-stick cooking sheet.


Icing: ¼ cup of white sugar + ¼ cup of cream (or milk) + 1T of butter. Cook till it forms into softball. Cool and beat to spread out consistency. If its took thick add a little more cream for spreading.
